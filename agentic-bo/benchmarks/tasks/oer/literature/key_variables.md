# Key Variables

Variables likely to matter:

- relative elemental fractions across the six-component simplex
- whether the composition sits near known active transition-metal oxide regions
- whether rare-earth content acts as a stabilizing or activity-modulating component rather than a dominant active phase
- whether the search discovers disconnected active regions rather than only smooth improvements around a known baseline

Reasonable literature-grounded expectations:

- Ni-Fe and Ni-Co families are credible OER baselines in alkaline media
- Fe often appears in highly active mixed-oxide or oxyhydroxide OER systems
- Ce-containing regions are plausible to explore because Ce-rich activity has been reported in adjacent multimetal OER searches

For this benchmark, the agent should reason at the level of composition, constraints, and search strategy rather than mechanism-specific claims.
