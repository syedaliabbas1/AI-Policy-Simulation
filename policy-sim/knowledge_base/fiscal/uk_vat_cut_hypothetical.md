# Hypothetical: UK VAT Cut from 20% to 17.5%

## Note

This is a hypothetical counter-scenario used exclusively for Stage 4 internal consistency verification. It is not a real policy proposal.

## Scenario

A hypothetical UK government reduces the standard rate of VAT from 20% back to 17.5%, effective immediately. All other policy parameters (benefits, spending, income tax) remain unchanged.

## Purpose in Simulation

Used to verify that archetype reasoning is internally consistent: if a VAT rise produces opposition, a VAT cut should produce support, and `support_or_oppose` scores should have opposite signs. This tests that the simulation engine is reasoning about the policy direction correctly rather than producing fixed responses.
